Claire Furino

Rutgers Bootcamp

My name is Claire Furino and I am 19 years old. I was born and raised in Hunterdon County, New Jersey. I live with both of my parents and an amazing cat and dog. I enjoy spending my free time surfing, cooking, and coding. I am very excited to see what I can learn at this bootcamp.




I became interested in coding after I attended the NJ Governor's School of Engineering and Technology my junior year summer. I spent five weeks taking classes at Rutgers including Nanofabrication, Quantum Physics and Intro to Robotics. I also worked on a research project and eventually created a pillowcase that monitors sleep cycles through conductive capacitive e-textile pressure sensors.
